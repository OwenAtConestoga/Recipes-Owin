Owen's Baked Chicken

Ingredients to Gather:
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Rosmary
  • Lemons!
    •
    Instructions:
    1. Cut up your chicken (whole chicken, breasts, etc)
    2. Pat it dry with a paper towel
    3. Season with all of the above
    4. Prepare a dish to put it the oven
    5. Preheat your oven to 450
    6. Bake chicken for 30-35 minutes
    7. Check until it reaches 162 internal
